About The Team

We are all CD students with a variety of focuses and backgrounds. Together, we are specializing in Web Design, Game Design and Visual Design. With this variety, we were able to contribute a variety of skills to the project. Early on in the ideation stage, we came up with a variety of website topics. We ultimately decided on an exercise page. We felt that there was a lot of creative freedom and opportunities with a topic like that. We are ultimately very happy and proud of the website that we produced.
