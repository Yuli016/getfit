About The Website

Our website was created to expose students to at home workouts so they can quickly exercise and still have enough time to study and work on school work. We recognize how important it is to prioritize physical activity in a students schedule. Positive physical and mental health has been proven to increase students work ethic, motivation, and ultimately grades. Feel free to explore and learn a little more about yourself and your body!


Balance

Balance training has been shown to prevent re-injury and reduce the risk of ligament problems in athletes.


Strength

May enhance your quality of life and improve your ability to do everyday activities. Building muscle also can contribute to better balance and may reduce your risk of falls.


Flexibility

Activities that stretch muscles can help you prevent injuries, back pain, and balance problems. A well-stretched muscle more easily achieves its full range of motion.


Endurance

Keeps your heart, lungs and circulatory system healthy and improves your overall fitness. As a result, can reduce the risk of many diabetes, heart disease and stroke.
